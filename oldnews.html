Back from SfN! I gave a poster on our FEF microstimulation results. It turns out FEF microstimulation can modulate the gain of a brainstem sensorimotor reflex. This kind of motif - top-down regulation of lower-level circuitry - might be a powerful way for the prefrontal cortex to exert control. You can check out the poster here.
